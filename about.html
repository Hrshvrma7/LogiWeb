About Us

Your Trusted Partner in Supply Chain Excellence

Founded in 2024 and headquartered in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, Sadhi Logistics Pvt. Ltd. is a trusted name in providing end-to-end logistics and supply chain solutions across India.

We understand that logistics is more than transportation—it is the backbone of every successful business. Our services are designed to provide speed, transparency, flexibility, and efficiency, enabling our clients to focus on growth while we manage their logistics seamlessly.

"Our vision is to become a leading logistics partner in India, recognized for integrity, innovation, and efficiency in every aspect of our operations."

Core Specialties

  • Customized Logistic Services: Tailored solutions designed to match the unique requirements of different industries.
  • Cargo Transportation: Safe and timely delivery of goods across Pan India through our well-established network.
  • Warehousing & Storage: Secure, scalable, and strategically located facilities for effective inventory management.
  • Supply Chain Management: End-to-end solutions that optimize operations, reduce costs, and enhance efficiency.
